# Case Study Template

Use this template to create additional case studies in the future.

## Case Study Ideas

1. **Graduate School Scenario**
   - Undergrad debt + grad school debt
   - Shows compound effect
   - Income-driven repayment plans

2. **Trade School Success**
   - Electrician/plumber/HVAC
   - Low debt, high income
   - 2-year program

3. **Military/Veteran Path**
   - GI Bill benefits
   - Zero debt scenario
   - Service commitment

4. **Parent PLUS Loan Scenario**
   - Parents borrowing for child
   - Retirement impact
   - Family financial strain

5. **Part-Time/Working Adult**
   - Employer tuition assistance
   - Slower pace, less debt
   - Career advancement

6. **Scholarship Athlete**
   - Full ride scenario
   - Minimal loans for living expenses
   - Best-case scenario

7. **Out-of-State Public**
   - Higher costs than in-state
   - Comparison to in-state option
   - When it's worth it vs. not

## Template Structure

### 1. Persona Card
- Name (use a common first name)
- Age
- Hometown
- School(s)
- Major
- Family situation (EFC)
- Goal/Dream

### 2. School Costs
- Tuition & Fees
- Room & Board
- Books & Supplies
- Personal Expenses
- **Total Cost of Attendance**

### 3. Year-by-Year Breakdown
For each year:
- Total cost
- Family contribution
- Grants/scholarships
- Work income
- **Loans needed**
- **Actual loans taken**
- **Remaining gap** (how they covered it)

### 4. At Graduation
- Total borrowed (by loan type)
- Accrued interest
- **Total owed**

### 5. Repayment Plan
- Monthly payment
- Total interest over life of loan
- Total amount repaid
- Debt-free date

### 6. After Graduation
- Job title
- Starting salary
- Monthly take-home
- Loan payment as % of income
- Quality of life impact

### 7. Key Decisions
List 5-7 smart or poor decisions made

### 8. Lessons Learned
What readers should take away

### 9. Calculator Link
Pre-filled scenario link

## Writing Tips

1. **Use real numbers** from actual schools
2. **Be specific** about decisions and consequences
3. **Show the math** - tables and breakdowns
4. **Include emotions** - stress, relief, regret
5. **End with lessons** - what would they do differently?
6. **Link to resources** - related guides and tools

## File Naming

- Main file: `case-study-[name].html`
- Use lowercase, hyphens for spaces
- Keep names short and memorable

## Example Scenarios

### Trade School (Excellent)
- **School:** Local community college HVAC program
- **Cost:** $8,000 total
- **Debt:** $5,000
- **Salary:** $55,000
- **Outcome:** Debt-free in 2 years

### Graduate School (Challenging)
- **Undergrad:** $30,000
- **Grad School:** $60,000
- **Total:** $90,000
- **Salary:** $75,000
- **Outcome:** 20-year repayment, but higher earning potential

### Out-of-State (Warning)
- **School:** Out-of-state flagship
- **Cost:** $50,000/year
- **Debt:** $80,000
- **Salary:** $50,000
- **Outcome:** Should have gone in-state

---

**Remember:** The goal is to educate, not judge. Show real consequences of real decisions.
