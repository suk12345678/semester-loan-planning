Frequently Asked Questions

Why is your calculator better than NerdWallet's?

While NerdWallet's calculator is good for basic estimates, ours goes much further:

  • We show you how interest accumulates during school, not just the final number
  • We let you plan in-school payments and one-time payments
  • We provide interactive charts and full amortization schedules
  • We have zero ads and don't track you
  • We let you compare scenarios side-by-side

Should I use the Federal Loan Simulator instead?

Use both! The Federal Loan Simulator is excellent for exploring Income-Driven Repayment plans after you graduate. Our calculator is better for:

  • Planning before and during school
  • Understanding how interest accumulates
  • Modeling private loans alongside federal loans
  • Testing in-school payment strategies

Is your calculator really free?

Yes! Completely free, no sign-up required, no ads, no tracking. We built this because we believe students deserve better tools to understand their debt.

How accurate is your calculator?

Our calculator uses the same formulas as loan servicers to calculate interest and payments. The accuracy depends on the accuracy of your inputs. We recommend:

  • Using your actual loan amounts and interest rates
  • Checking your loan types (subsidized vs. unsubsidized vs. private)
  • Verifying your disbursement schedule with your school
