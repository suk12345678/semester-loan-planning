Why Case Studies Matter

Numbers and percentages are abstract. Real stories help you understand the long-term impact of borrowing decisions.

These case studies show three different paths through college - from the smart community college transfer to the cautionary tale of excessive private school debt.

Each case study includes:

  • Year-by-year cost breakdown
  • Actual loan amounts and types
  • Post-graduation salary and monthly payments
  • Interactive calculator link with pre-filled data
  • Key lessons and takeaways
